# First Wave Send Plan

Date: 2026-04-26

## Goal

Validate whether high-fit automation and agent-tooling teams will engage with the AI Workflow Risk Scorecard.

## First Wave

Send to 10 prospects only. Do not send the same day to the full 100-prospect list.

Recommended split:

- 8 automation agencies / n8n / Make partners.
- 2 MCP or agent-tooling product teams.

## Send Timing

Day 1:

- Send 5 messages.
- Wait 24 hours.
- Check for immediate replies, bounces, or obvious objections.

Day 2:

- Send the next 5 messages with any small wording adjustment learned from Day 1.

Day 5 or 6:

- Send Follow-Up 1 only to non-repliers.

Day 10 or 11:

- Send Follow-Up 2 only to non-repliers.

## Pass Signals

This first 10-prospect batch is promising if any of the following happen:

- 2+ replies.
- 1+ prospect sends a score or asks for our opinion.
- 1+ prospect says the checklist would be useful for their clients.
- 1+ prospect corrects our wording with useful category language.

## Interpretation

If agencies reply more than product teams, continue with agency-led outreach and package the scorecard as a client discovery or pre-audit tool.

If product teams reply more than agencies, reposition toward MCP/tool-access readiness and partner with platforms.

If nobody replies, adjust the first sentence and subject line before sending to the broader list. Do not assume the offer is dead after only 10 sends.
