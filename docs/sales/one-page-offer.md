# One-Page Offer: AI Workflow Mini-Audit

## Headline

AI workflow security review for teams giving automation real access.

## Who It Is For

Teams using AI agents, MCP servers, n8n, Make, Zapier, internal LLM workflows, or custom automations that can read or write business data.

## Problem

AI workflows often start as small productivity experiments. Then they quietly gain access to email, CRM, invoices, files, Slack, APIs, or customer data. The risk is rarely the model alone. The risk is the access pattern around it: credentials, permissions, logs, approvals, and recovery.

## Offer

OctoBot Labs reviews one workflow or agent surface and produces a concise risk report.

## Deliverables

- Workflow and tool-access map
- Credential and permission review
- Approval-gate review
- Audit-trail and logging review
- Data-handling and provider-exposure notes
- Recovery and fallback review
- 3-7 prioritized findings
- Fix-now and fix-later backlog

## Price

EUR 750-1,200 fixed fee.

## Timeline

5 business days after complete materials.

## Inputs Needed

- Sanitized workflow export, screenshots, or walkthrough
- Tool list and permission summary
- Description of read/write actions
- Known concerns from the workflow owner

## Boundaries

This is a technical workflow review. It is not legal advice, certification, penetration testing, or a security guarantee.
