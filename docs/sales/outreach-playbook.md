# OctoBot Labs Outreach Playbook

## Positioning

OctoBot Labs helps teams review AI agents and automation workflows before they touch real credentials, tools, and customer data.

## First Target Segments

- AI automation agencies
- SaaS teams launching MCP servers
- n8n, Make, Zapier consultants with business clients
- Small B2B SaaS companies advertising AI agents
- Founder-led companies using internal AI automations

## Prospect Signals

- Mentions AI agents or MCP on website
- Publishes n8n/Make/Zapier automation services
- Shows integrations with Gmail, Slack, CRM, accounting, or payments
- Offers AI workflow implementation
- Has a public changelog or docs for agent tooling

## LinkedIn Or Community DM

Saw your work on {{specific_signal}}. OctoBot Labs is reviewing AI automations for teams that are starting to give agents real tool access. The checks are practical: credentials, scopes, approvals, logs, and recovery. Happy to do one mini-audit if you want a second set of eyes.

## Qualification Questions

- What does the workflow do?
- Which tools can it call?
- Can it read private customer or business data?
- Can it write, send, delete, approve, or purchase anything?
- Where are credentials stored?
- Are tool calls and approvals logged?
- What happens if the workflow behaves incorrectly?
