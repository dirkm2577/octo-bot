# Monitoring Retainer Scope

The monitoring retainer keeps AI workflow risk visible as clients add tools, credentials, models, and automation paths.

## Monthly Fee

- Starter: EUR 750/month
- Standard: EUR 1,250/month
- Extended: EUR 1,500/month

## Included Work

### Starter

- Review up to 2 workflow changes per month
- Monthly permission drift checklist
- Monthly risk log update
- Async questions up to 60 minutes/month
- Monthly summary

### Standard

- Review up to 5 workflow changes per month
- Monthly permission drift checklist
- Review new AI tools, providers, MCP servers, or major prompt/tool changes
- Async questions up to 2 hours/month
- Monthly summary and prioritized action list

### Extended

- Review up to 8 workflow changes per month
- Monthly permission drift checklist
- Quarterly mini-readout call
- Async questions up to 4 hours/month
- Monthly summary, prioritized action list, and evidence-pack update

## Exclusions

- Unlimited implementation
- 24/7 incident response
- Legal advice or compliance certification
- Full penetration testing
- Review of unrelated systems outside agreed workflows
- Handling live secrets through normal email or chat

## Change Review Inputs

For each change, the client provides:

- What changed
- Systems touched
- New read/write actions
- New credentials or permission changes
- New data categories
- Screenshots, sanitized exports, or a short walkthrough

## Monthly Output

- Workflow changes reviewed
- New or changed risks
- Permission drift notes
- Recommended actions
- Items requiring legal, data protection, or specialist security review
